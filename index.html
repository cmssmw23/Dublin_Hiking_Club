About Dublin Hiking Club

Welcome to the website for the Dublin Hiking Club. We have about 300 members, mainly from the Dublin region but also from all corners of the country

As we are a mainly Dublin-based club, we do most of our walking in the Wicklow and Dublin mountains area, but we also take regular trips to other parts of Ireland.

Club members are involved in a wide range of hillwalking, hiking, climbing activities, and also frequently organise trips away.
